The Best Movies According to Zhen Zong

My top 3 movie of all-time

Dying to Survive

Dying to Survive is a 2018 Chinese comedy-drama film[3] directed by Wen Muye in his feature film debut. The film is based on the real-life story of Lu Yong (陆勇), a Chinese leukemia patient who smuggled cheap and generic cancer medicine from India for 1,000 Chinese cancer sufferers in 2004.[4][5] Dying to Survive stars Xu Zheng in the lead role, who also co-produced the film with Ning Hao.

My comment

When I was a child, I passed by a drugstore. The couplet on the door read, "I just wish there would be no disease in the world, why worry about the medicine on the shelf staining the dust?"

Parasite

The struggling Kim family sees an opportunity when the son starts working for the wealthy Park family. Soon, all of them find a way to work within the same household and start living a parasitic life.

My comment

The greatest thing about this movie is that the rich actually did nothing wrong. Their husbands and wives are loving, they have happy children, there is no cheating, no cold violence, no child abuse, and they are generous and not stingy to their drivers and servants. They They are really kind and simple, but you just can't help but hate them, hate them, hate them.

The Secret Life of Walter Mitty

A man living a dull life dreams up romantic and action-filled scenarios in order to escape from monotony. When his own job is threatened, he sets out on a real romantic and action-packed journey.

My comment

I use my sleepy fantasies to fill the boring gap. I have been mediocre for many years, but I don’t know that one day I will abandon the old routine and practice traveling far away to encounter the beauty of the world. From Greenland to Iceland to the Himalayas, the scenery is majestic and full of contagion. People who have experiences and stories will have a new state of mind towards life and love. The warm and inspiring movie chicken soup always gives people an illusory feeling of fullness.
